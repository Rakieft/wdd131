Local Business Website Plan

Recyclonet

Site Name

Example Business Local

This name represents a local business focused on providing services to the community. It is easy to remember and reflects the essence of the business.

Optional domain availability: examplebusinesslocal.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the website is to provide information about the services offered by Example Business Local, including contact details, service descriptions, pricing, and a booking system.

Scenarios

  • What services do you offer for home repair?
  • How can I schedule an appointment with a technician?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #3498db (Used for headings and accents)

Secondary Color: #2ecc71 (Used for buttons and highlights)

Background Color: #ecf0f1 (Used for the overall background)

Typography

Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif (Used for all text on the website)

Headings Font: Arial, sans-serif (Used for headings such as h1, h2, and h3)
